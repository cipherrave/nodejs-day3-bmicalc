Body Mass Index (BMI) Calculation:

BMI is a measure of a person’s leanness or corpulence based on their height and weight. It quantifies tissue mass and is widely used to determine whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height.

The resulting BMI value falls into different categories:

Underweight: BMI < 18.5

Normal weight: BMI 18.5 - 25

Overweight: BMI 25 - 30

Obese

  • (Class I) BMI 30 - 35
  • (Class II) BMI 35 - 40
  • (Class III): BMI > 40

    • Remember that BMI has limitations and doesn’t account for muscle mass, bone density, or other factors. Consult a healthcare professional for a comprehensive assessment

    Healthy Habits for a Better Life:

    Regular Exercise 🏃‍♂️🏃‍♀️🏋️🚵‍♀️

    Engage in physical activity to boost physical health, reduce stress, and improve stamina.

    Balanced Diet 🍆🍎🥦🥬

    Consume a variety of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and healthy fats.

    Adequate Sleep 😴💤

    Prioritize quality and constant sleep to rejuvenate both your mind and body.

    Hydration 💧💧

    Proper hydration supports metabolism, skin health, and overall vitality.

    Limit Screen Time 💻📱⌛

    Reduce daily screen time to prevent mental fatigue and promote better sleep.

    Mindfulness Practices 🧘🧘‍♀️📿

    Practice meditation, deep breathing, or yoga to manage stress and enhance emotional balance.

    Social Connections 🙋‍♀️🙋‍♂️🤦

    Foster relationships with friends and family for better mental health.

    Avoid Excessive Alcohol and Tobacco 🍶🍸🍷🍺📉

    These habits can negatively affect both physical and mental health.

    Learn New Skills ✨🏫📖

    Engage in activities that challenge your mind and enhance cognitive abilities.

    Remember, small positive changes in daily habits accumulate over time, leading to a happier, healthier you. Consistency is key! 🌟
